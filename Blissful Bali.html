Blissful Bali

Day Activity
Day 1 On your arrival at Denpasar International airport in Bali, you will be received by the agent's representative and escorted to the hotel. Complete the registration process at the hotel and freshen up.
Now, you have ample time to relax at the hotel. At night, doze off to sound sleep at the hotel.
Day 2 Have a healthy breakfast at the hotel and get ready to indulge in pleasurable watersports at Nusa Dua. You will be taken to Nusa Dua for this ecstatic play with water.
You can take up watersports such as snorkeling, parasailing, snorkeling, and jet ski. Post this water-indulgence, refuel yourself with a lavish lunch at an Indian restaurant (charges extra). Continue your day with a shopping trip to the local market and buy home traditional souvenirs.
Also visit Uluwatu Temple, a famous Balinese Hindu temple built in unique architectural style.
Post sunset enjoy Kecak Fire Dance show that depicts Ramayana's snippet. The constant chanting “Kecak-Kecak” makes the dance incredible. No wonder, the title is perfectly onomatopoeic.
A fire activity towards the closure of the performance enhances your experience.
Conclude this day of your Bali family holiday package by heading to Kuta Beach. After having a whale of time there with your loved ones, return to your hotel for a relaxing overnight stay.
Day 3 Wake up to a refreshing morning and savor a scrumptious breakfast with your family. Today, you will be moving to Seminyak. So, check-out from the hotel and enjoy a comfortable trip. On reaching Seminyak, check-in to the hotel and freshen up. Now, it is time to head to Kintamani as the spirit and energy of volcano and Lake Batur beckons you.
You can add to this Bali family holiday by taking up a cycling tour of the volcano (charges extra). Paddling while discovering the exotic natural beauty and breathing the tropical fragrance in the loving company of your family is an experience to treasure.
After an amazing day, return to your hotel in Kintamani and hop on your beds for a relaxing sleep.
Day 4 Arise to a beautiful morning where a delectable breakfast adds health and flavor to your day. Now, you have the entire day at leisure as per this Bali family package.
You can set off for an architectural city tour, admiring the intricate details of centuries old edifices to abstract designs of modern era. Take a glimpse of Balinese architectural prowess at cathedrals, churches, museums, and monuments. (Charges extra)
To get close to nature, you can head to any of the National Parks on this day of your Bali family vacation package. Saunter through these protected areas with your family. Ride a horse, go for a hike, or just delight in the scenic splendor all around. (Charges extra)
Once you have enjoyed your day to the fullest, indulge in some rejuvenating spa therapies. Surrender your body and soul to holistic healing treatments for two hours.
After a happening day, return to your hotel and step into dream world for the night.
Day 5 Enjoy a hearty breakfast and pack your belongings. Your check out is followed by your transfer to airport from where you will board your flight back home.
