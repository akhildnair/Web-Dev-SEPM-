Extraordinary Orlando/New York

Day Activity
Day 1 Welcome to the dream city of Disney World and NASA, Orlando. On your arrival at the international airport in Orlando, you will be received by our tour representative. Enjoy being warmly welcomed, and proceed for transfer to the hotel booked for your luxurious stay in Orlando.
As soon as you arrive at the hotel, complete all check-in formalities, and unwind in your room. The remaining part of the day is free for you to relax, and enjoy the lit night views of the magical city.
Enjoy a restful overnight stay at the hotel.
Day 2 After a lip-smacking American breakfast, proceed to explore one of the 10 National Aeronautics and Space Administration field centers, the Kennedy Space Complex.
Today's, Discover KSC Tour provides an opportunity to the travelers to explore NASA's primary launch center of human spaceflight, and also understand the future of space travel through presentations.
After exploring the Kennedy Space Centre, head back to the hotel for a comfortable overnight stay.
Day 3 Kickstart your day with a scrumptious breakfast, and brace yourselves for a thrilling tour of the Kennedy Space Center.
Today, you will get a chance to be a part of the exciting world of space exploration at Kennedy Space Center Visitor Complex which features historic spacecraft and memorabilia, and a range of bus tours of the spaceport.
Later, visit the Rocket Garden which houses various missiles, sounding rockets, and space launch vehicles. Here, you can photograph the actual rockets, spaceships, spacecraft, and spacesuits from the Mercury, Gemini, and Apollo programs, and gather knowledge about NASAâ€™s space heroes.
Day 4 Enjoy a healthy breakfast at the hotel, and proceed to explore the Kennedy Space Complex for one last time.
Today, you will get an opportunity to spectate IMAX space films, experience all of KSC's exhibits & shows, go on a bus tour and visit the U.S. Astronaut Hall of Fame. Later, in the afternoon, you will get a chance to interact with the astronauts of NASA, and meet a veteran member of NASA's Astronaut Corps.
After a thrilling day, head back to hotel for a restful overnight stay.
Day 5 Post breakfast, gear up for a visit to the wonderful, and exciting dream world of Disneyland.
Today, you will be treated to a mesmerizing experience as you embark on a tour to the dreamy world of the enchanting Disneyland of Orlando. This tour will allow total access to the Magic Kingdom in Disney World where you will get an opportunity to spectate various shows and enjoy breathtaking character experiences such as that of Mickey Mouse, Ariel, Cinderella, Tinker Bell, and more.
After a rejuvenating day at the Disney World, head back to the hotel for an overnight stay.
Day 6 After a filling breakfast, you will be taken on a tour to another exhilarating theme park of Orlando, the Universal Studio.
Owned by NBCUniversal, the Universal Studio is an American theme park which is known for its innovative rides, attractions and is designed to let visitors experience the movies like never before.
Tourists will get an opportunity to explore each and every aspect of the Universal Orlando, be it theme parks, dining experiences, shopping areas, entertainment, events, and eye-catching shows.
Later, head back to the hotel for an overnight stay.
Day 7 After a delectable breakfast, get ready for a visit to the SeaWorld.
Today, you will be taken on an awe-inspiring journey where wonders exist beyond the ocean's door. Enjoy a fun-filled day at SeaWorld where you can spectate various shows such as the Sensational Shamu, Believe, and Blue Horizon.
After an exciting day, head back to the hotel for an overnight stay.
Day 8 Enjoy a flavorful breakfast meal before departing from Orlando for your onward journey to New York.
These USA east coast tours welcome you to one of world's major commercial, financial and cultural centers. This iconic city is known for its towering Skyscrapers, high-end shopping, vibrant nightlife, bustling Times Square, sprawling Central Park, marvelous Empire State, and much more.
On your arrival at the airport, you will be received by our representative. He will escort you to the hotel booked for your stay in New York. Check-in at the hotel, and enjoy a luxurious overnight stay.
Day 9 After a palatable breakfast, head out for a city tour of New York.
Visit the icon of New York, 'The Statue of Liberty, which is also referred to as the Goddess of Freedom. Dig into the glorious history of this 100-year old statue.
Proceed forward for a visit One World Tower Observation Deck, located at the floors 100-102 of One World Trade Center. Cherish the spectacular bird-eye views of the New York City, and enjoy the breathtaking experiences that lie within the tower such as a thrilling 47 second ride to the topmost floor, history of New York, and luxury dining.
After the conclusion of the city tour, head back to the hotel for an overnight stay.
Day 10 Enjoy the final breakfast of your USA tour, and check-out from the hotel. Our representative will escort you to the airport from where you can board your flight back home.
