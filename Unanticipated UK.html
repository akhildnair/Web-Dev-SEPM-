Unanticipated United Kingdom

Day Activity
Day 1 Upon arrival at the London Heathrow Airport(LHR), our team member will welcome you at the airport and then transfer to the hotel in a private vehicle.
On reaching the hotel, you will check in and freshen up. You can utilize the rest of the leisure day exploring the city on own or simply relaxing at the hotel. Night stay will be at the hotel.
Day 2 After breakfast in the morning, you will head out for a trip to Madame Tussauds where you will witness wax statues of celebrities and famous personalities from all over the world.
You can click photos with your favorite celebrity wax statue and make a memorable day. In addition, you can also watch 4D movies of Marvel superheroes in action.
After ending the tour, you will return to the hotel for night stay.
Day 3 Indulge in a tasty breakfast meal and get ready to explore the city. Go for a Hop-on-Hop-off City Bus Tour of London.
These routes cover most of the popular monuments in the city including top museums, churches, and chapels. You will also get a free river cruise between Westminster Pier and Greenwich.
After the end of the wonderful day, you will return back to the hotel for night stay.
Day 4 After a nice breakfast in the morning, you will proceed to Coca-Cola London eye for a flight over Thames River to eye-witness the best panoramic view of the city.
You will also see Palaces, churches and other landmarks which will give a unique perspective of London City. You can also enjoy 4D experiences on this trip before heading back to the hotel for night stay.
Day 5 In the morning, after breakfast, you will sign out from the hotel and transfer to Edinburgh. On arrival, you will check into the hotel and spend the rest of the evening leisure time on your own.
You can take a short walk around the city or enjoy delicious foods at the local restaurants. Afterward, you will return to the hotel for night stay.
Day 6 After a yummy breakfast, you will get ready to explore the Edinburgh Castle with guides. The castle built on remains of an extinct volcano is one of the most preserved heritage sites in Scotland.
The castle has many historic tales and it is probably the most attacked Castle during the different time frame. You will also visit Rock Castle where tales about hanging people is popular.
Later in the evening, you will have leisure time to enjoy at own. Return to the hotel after an amazing day for a cozy overnight sleep.
Day 7 After a morning breakfast, you will proceed for a day trip of the city. You will board a bus for the tour of Waterloo Palace in central Edinburgh, Princess Street, and Grassmarket.
You will then make your way to Royal Mile with brief information on Old Town. The tour will also cover Quaint Dean Village, St Mary's Cathedral, Burns Monument, Holyrood Park. Proceeding ahead, pay a visit to John Knox House, Scottish Storytelling Center, and Canongate.
After completing the tour, you will return to the hotel for night stay.
Day 8 You will have a tasty breakfast in the morning and then free to spend the rest of the leisure day on your own.
You can walk around the city and enjoy the weather. You can also go shopping and buy a souvenir for family and friends. You can interact with local people and learn more about the city.
At the end of the day, return to the hotel for a comfortable overnight stay.
Day 9 After breakfast, it's another leisure day to scout the city on own. You can use a map to track down the places to visit in the city.
Since the city is packed with ancient history, you will have a lot to explore even at own. You can spend the day as you wish to have fun and excitement. Later, night stay will be at the hotel.
Day 10 After breakfast, you will check out from the hotel and transfer to the airport. You will board a flight for the home with unforgettable memories from the tour.
