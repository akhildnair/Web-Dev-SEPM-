Magnificent Malaysia

Day Activity
Day 1 Arrive at Kuala Lumpur Airport, from where you will be transferred to Johor Bahru. On arrival, check into the hotel and relax for the remainder of the day.
Spend the night at the hotel.
Day 2 Today is sure to be one of the funnest days of the entire trip, since today is the day you get to go to LEGOLAND®. After breakfast, leave for the theme park, where you can spend the day enjoying rides and shows, before returning to the hotel towards the evening.
Day 3 After breakfast, check out the Johor Bahru hotel and transfer to Kuala Lumpur. On arrival, check into the hotel and relax for a while.
Towards the evening, it is time to leave for a night tour of this beautiful city. Cover all major attractions before returning to the hotel for the night.
Day 4 After a lovely breakfast at the hotel, it is time to leave for a day tour of Genting Highlands, with a one way cable car ride.
You also get to explore the Batu Caves, as part of the Malaysia tour package with LEGOLAND®. After winding up the day trip, return to the hotel for the night.
Day 5 After a hearty breakfast, it is time to leave for the airport, since this is the last day of your Malaysia tour package with LEGOLAND® theme park. Catch your flight back home, with plenty of memories to keep you company.
