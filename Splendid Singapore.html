Splendid Singapore

Day Activity
Day 1 Arrival at Singapore Changi International Airport(SIN), our representative will meet you at the airport and escort you to the Hotel Marina Bay Sands
Later, enjoy night safari to come face to face with wildlife in their natural habitat. Return to the hotel for the night.
Day 2 Post breakfast, leave for an exhaustive tour of Sentosa, and enjoy a cable car ride. At Sentosa, you can enjoy a plethora of activities and entertainment options.
You will also get to explore S.E.A. Aquarium, and enjoy the Wings Of Time show at night. Later, return to your hotel for the night.
Day 3 After a lovely breakfast, it is time to leave for the planned day itinerary, which is a day spent exploring the many nuances of Universal Studios. From rides to shows, you can partake in a lot of enjoyment here.
Later, return to the hotel for the night.
Day 4 One of the biggest highlights of this tour is this day trip to Mount Faber. With this Singapore package, you get to travel on the famous Pokemon themed Singapore Cable Car to the peak.
After an exciting day spent here, return to your hotel for the night.
Day 5 Enjoy your breakfast, after which you check out of the hotel and transfer to the airport.
Once there, catch your flight back home.
